🏆 Top 5 Tips to Maximise Your Ad ROI

  • Add real photos ASAP — Ads with responsive display images get 3× more conversions. Even 5 real decoration photos help massively.
  • Reply within 30 minutes — Leads go cold fast. The team that calls back first wins the booking 80% of the time.
  • Pause ads on days you're fully booked — Never pay for leads you can't fulfil. Set ad schedules in Google Ads.
  • Run ads 8 AM–8 PM only — Event planning decisions happen during the day. Save budget by scheduling off overnight.
  • Track which keywords convert — After 2 weeks, pause keywords with 0 conversions and increase budgets on winners.
