Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our event decoration services

How far in advance should I book? ＋

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for most events. For weddings or large events, 2–3 months ahead is ideal. However, we do accommodate last-minute bookings subject to availability — call us and we'll do our best!

What areas in Bangalore do you cover? ＋

We cover all areas of Bangalore including Whitefield, Koramangala, HSR Layout, Indiranagar, Jayanagar, BTM Layout, Electronic City, Sarjapura, Marathahalli, Bannerghatta Road, and more. No area is too far!

Are there any hidden charges? ＋

Absolutely not. We believe in 100% transparent pricing. Before any booking is confirmed, we provide a detailed breakdown of all costs. What we quote is exactly what you pay — no surprises on the day of your event.

What is the minimum decoration package price? ＋

Our decoration packages start from ₹4500. The final cost depends on the event type, theme, number of guests, and services included. Contact us for a free, personalised quote tailored to your budget.

Do you handle outdoor events? ＋

Yes! We specialise in both indoor and outdoor event decoration and catering. Whether it's a terrace birthday party, a garden wedding, or a hall celebration — our team is fully equipped to handle any venue in Bangalore.
